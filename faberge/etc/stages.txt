  va spazzolati. eh rathchet with rhythmicized enveloping, vn spazzolati with
  rhythmicized enveloping, va spazzolati with rhythmicized enveloping.

  upperregister clarinet, bowed crotale, violin touch-four harmonic; va tremolo
  flautando on low fundamental, vc tremolo flautando on low fundamental; pf ff
  lowregister strike activating silently depressed harmonic field. pf ff
  lowregister strike descends stepwise during segment. pf silently depressed
  harmonic field ascends stepwise during segment.

* broken shell (β) culmination: oboe doppeltrill. idea of coruscating climax
  given so early in the piece: doppeltrill is achieved but subject to a process
  of dynamic enveloping that makes the material recede and reappear and recede
  and reappear while other materials progress and overlap. culmination: oboe
  doppeltrill, lateral piano A0 scrape, no crotales, sustained fl flz,
  sustained clarinet, sustained lowregister vc note played extremely
  ponticello, sustained midregister vn, va pochissimo scratch pedal.

enveloped with a swell followed by a short rest followed by a quiet stroke of

played at as loud a dynamic as possible without revealing the mallet attacks.

vn, va sputter gesture of starting up: triple-struck rimbalzando followed by

triple-struck rimbalzando followed by two or three tremoli flautandi in

vc sputter gesture of starting up: pair of 1/2 clt sfz strokes with lh
glissando at a width of a major second up and down followed by tremolo alla
punta (non flautando) scored with a crescendo. progresses to pair of 1/2 clt
sfz glissando strokes followed by two or three waves of swelled tremolo alla
punta. progresses to same 1/2 clt initiation followed by countless waves of

increase to a major ninth during development of material. progresses to include
oscilliandi harmonichi ponticelli after 1/2 clt glissando strokes and before
